Instructions for each application modality

Online

If you’re using your logo for any digital purpose — from PowerPoint presentations to social media profiles — choose PNG files.

Print

If you’re sending your logo to be printed on business cards, brochures, clothing, and more, provide the printer with a PDF or EPS. If the print is more technical and requires edits from the printer, send vector files (SVG or EPS).

Sending to a Designer

Submit in SVG or EPS format.

Sharing

When sharing a logo file, send a PDF or PNG, as they’re easy-to-read file formats.

Resizing

Don’t see all of the file sizes you need? Use this website to adjust the file sizes of your PNG logo.

We preserve the essence of the original logo by carefully enhancing it. Every element has been refined to highlight the brand identity. The result is a renewed and modern logo that maintains the authenticity of the original. The idea is to strengthen the brand and consequently the company in the market.

4. Trello

Acessar

Trello is a workflow management platform that assists in prioritizing, tracking, and validating completed tasks. It provides a visual interface with customizable boards, lists, and cards, allowing teams to organize their projects effectively. The platform offers features such as due dates, checklists, and labels to enhance task management. Teams can collaborate in real-time, assign tasks, and track progress, fostering transparency and accountability. With its intuitive interface and flexible structure, Trello simplifies project management, improves team coordination, and boosts productivity.

5. Google Analytics

Acessar

Google Analytics enables businesses to track and analyze user interactions on their website, providing valuable insights to optimize performance. It measures engagement metrics like page views, session duration, and bounce rates, helping understand visitor behavior. Conversion tracking allows evaluating marketing effectiveness and goal completion. For example, e-commerce websites can identify top-selling product pages to optimize strategies. Additionally, Google Analytics provides demographic and interest data, aiding in tailoring content and campaigns to target audiences. In summary, it's a powerful tool for businesses to gain insights, enhance user experiences, and drive growth.
